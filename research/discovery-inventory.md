### Recommended taxonomy

1. **Learning substrate** — psychological safety, knowledge creation, absorptive capacity, telemetry.
2. **Learning mechanism** — feedback loops, experimentation, retrospectives, error correction, exploration/exploitation.
3. **Adaptation target** — people, workflows, software, organizational structure, strategy, or business model.
4. **Agency level** — human-led, AI-assisted, agent-operated, bounded-autonomous, or fully autonomous.
5. **Loop closure** — insight only, recommendation, approved action, automatic action, or self-modification.
6. **Governance** — auditability, permissions, reversibility, evaluation, escalation, and human override.
7. **Outcome** — productivity, quality, speed, resilience, innovation, or unit economics.
