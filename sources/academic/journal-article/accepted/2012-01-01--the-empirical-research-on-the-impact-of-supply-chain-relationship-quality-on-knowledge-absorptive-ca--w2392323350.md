# The Empirical Research on the Impact of Supply Chain Relationship Quality on Knowledge Absorptive Capacity and Cooperation Innovation Performance

## Relevance decision

Title directly studies absorptive capacity, an established organizational learning and knowledge-conversion construct.

## Preserved evidence

Relationship quality between enterprises affected absorptive capacity and acquisitive capacity of knowledge directly,and absorptive capacity is the key factor for technological innovation.Based on supply chain,this study established a conceptual model which is the impact of relationship quality on knowledge absorptive capacity and cooperation innovation performance,distinguished between potential absorptive capacity and realized absorptive capacity,and discussed the differences of the impact between business with suppliers and business with customers.The result showed the relationship quality had a positive impact on potential absorptive capacity;absorptive capacity had a positive impact on innovation performance,realized absorption capacity significantly affected the cooperation performance;the relationship quality between enterprises and their suppliers had a positive impact on innovation performance only,the relationship quality between enterprises and their customer had a positive impact on innovation performance and cooperative performance.
