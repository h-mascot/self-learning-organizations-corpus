# Study on Relationship between Knowledge Absorptive Capacity and Incubating Enterprise Performance

## Relevance decision

Title directly studies absorptive capacity, an established organizational learning and knowledge-conversion construct.

## Preserved evidence

This paper considers the relationship among the enterprise knowledge absorptive capacity,the influencing factors of knowledge absorptive capacity,incubating enterprise performance to form a new analysis framework and put forward some hypothesis and a conceptual model.The main proposition is that the knowledge base of enterprise,the RD investment of enterprise,the learning force and methods of enterprise,enterprise learning mechanism affect the knowledge absorptive capacity,meanwhile,the enterprise knowledge absorptive capacity promotes the incubating enterprise performance.
