# The Relationship of the Intellectual Capital and Technology Innovation——Taking Absorptive Capacity as Moderator

## Relevance decision

Title directly studies absorptive capacity, an established organizational learning and knowledge-conversion construct.

## Preserved evidence

In this paper,research findings on intellectual capital,absorptive capacity and technology innovation are reviewed.And then the conceptual model for assessing the correlation between intellectual capital and absorptive capacity on technology innovation performance is developed on the basis pf various hypothesis.The results shows that various components of intellectual capital are interactive;intellectual capital and absorptive capacity have significant effect on technology innovation performance;absorptive capacity enhance the positive effect of human capital and social capital on technology innovation performance.
