# The Determinants of FDI Spillover

## Relevance decision

Title/abstract directly connects an organizational unit or business context with learning, knowledge, adaptation, innovation, or improvement.

## Preserved evidence

Based on the network approach,the main motivation of the paper is to analysis the determinants of FDI spillover from the questionnaire surveys.It showed that the role of foreign - owned subsidiaries significantly affects the knowledge spillover.It also found the communication within knowledge,product,management and marketing with the local firms is the key channel of knowledge spillover instead of labor turnover in China.Thus,it is very important to setup the network platform to facilitate the multi -tiers exchanges between domestic firms and foreign subsidiaries.Some other results include:(1 ) the spillover effect is more significant for subsidiary with appropriate technology;(2 ) the deeper cooperation with foreign - owned firm,the more spillover effect the local firm acquires;(3 ) the more spillover effect is from industrialized countries comparing with the other countries;(4) the spillover effect from non - sole foreign - funded firm is significant.
