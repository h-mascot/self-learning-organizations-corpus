# Study on Absorptive Capacity for Enterprises to Obtain Customer Knowledge Oriented CRM

## Relevance decision

Title directly studies absorptive capacity, an established organizational learning and knowledge-conversion construct.

## Preserved evidence

This paper studies the absorptive capacity for enterprises to aquire customer-related knowledge and introduces a conceptual framework of customer-related knowledge absorptive capacity.It also studies the four aspects of absorptive capacity:aquiring,assimilating,transforming and exploiting customer-related knowledge.The research shows that if enterprises possess the capacity of aquiring,assimilating,transforming and exploiting customer-related knowledge they will abtain both customer value and enterprise profit.
