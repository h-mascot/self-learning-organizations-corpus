# Theoretical Review of Absorptive Capacity

## Relevance decision

Title directly studies absorptive capacity, an established organizational learning and knowledge-conversion construct.

## Preserved evidence

Absorptive capacity is closely associated with knowledge acquisition,assimilation and exploitation,and the study of absorptive capacity theory has become one of the focuses recently.In this article,we summarize and review the concept and meaning,dimensions and measurement,process models,antecedent influencing factors,and contingent influencing factors of absorptive capacity.Furthermore,we explore some avenues of absorptive capacity in future research.
