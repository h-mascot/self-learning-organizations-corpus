# A Review on Import and Spillover of Technology in China from Perspective of Innovation Capacity

## Relevance decision

Title/abstract directly connects an organizational unit or business context with learning, knowledge, adaptation, innovation, or improvement.

## Preserved evidence

In the strategy background of ″ Enhancing the independent innovation capacity,building an innovation-oriented country″ in China,Reviewing the technology import from the view of innovation capacity is a useful reference for strategic path selection.The paper reviews the channels,effect and impact of technology import and spillover from the view of innovation capacity.The result shows that the technology import is still an important technological acquire sources for China,has a significant positive effect.However,the technology import also has many problems.So it's needed to form a virtuous cycle ″import-digestion and absorption-diffusion-further innovation″,through further adjustment policy and foster the absorptive capacity,achieve the transformation from simulation to independent innovation.By the way,about the research methods,the further research need to play more attention on the cases study on the regional and industry in order to resolve the specific and practical problems.
