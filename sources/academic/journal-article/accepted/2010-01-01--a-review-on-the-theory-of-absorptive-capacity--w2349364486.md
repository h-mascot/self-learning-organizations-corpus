# A Review on the Theory of Absorptive Capacity

## Relevance decision

Title directly studies absorptive capacity, an established organizational learning and knowledge-conversion construct.

## Preserved evidence

Absorptive Capacity is an capacity of acquisiting,assimilating,transforming,Exploiting external knowledge from outward organization for product innovation and technology innovation,and is also an important dynamic source of competitive advantage. On the prior research,this paper analyses the concept,dimensions and factors that affect absorptive capacity,and proposes the future research.
