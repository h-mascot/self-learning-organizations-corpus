# Study on Relationship between Knowledge Absorptive Capacity and Performance Based on the View of Corporate Entrepreneurship

## Relevance decision

Title directly studies absorptive capacity, an established organizational learning and knowledge-conversion construct.

## Preserved evidence

This article considers the relationships among the knowledge absorptive capacity,prior knowledge,organization coordination,RD investment and firm performance to form a new analysis framework and put forward a comprehensive model and hypothesis.The main proposition is that prior knowledge,organization coordination,RD investment affect the knowledge absorptive capacity of established enterprises and the knowledge absorptive capacity promotes the firm performance.
