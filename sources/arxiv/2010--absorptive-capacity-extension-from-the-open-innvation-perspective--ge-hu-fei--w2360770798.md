# Absorptive Capacity Extension from the Open Innvation Perspective

## Evidence span

> The organizational absorptive capacity to leverage external knowledge resource is extremely important source of competitive advantage. With the development of open innovation and the complexity of the relationship between organization and its external environment,the existing absorptive capability concept couldn't explain the organizational behavior and competitive advantage in the network context and in the open innovation model. In this article,we review the antecedents,processes,output and moderators of the absorptive capacity,and analysis the three dimensions and two stages of the open innovation. Based on that,we propose that we could extend the concept according to the process dynamics,explorative and exploitative learning,combinative characters of the knowledge and ego-network,and the paths of the knowledge transfer.
