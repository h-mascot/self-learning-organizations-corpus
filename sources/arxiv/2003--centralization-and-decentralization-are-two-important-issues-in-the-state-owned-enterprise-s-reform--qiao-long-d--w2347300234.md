# Centralization and Decentralization are Two Important Issues in the State-owned Enterprise's Reform

## Evidence span

> Centralization and decentralization are two important contents to construct the controlling power structure in state-owned enterprises and they deal with the total benefits of an enterprise and the benefis of the managers. After review of the reform in state-owned enterprises, gain and loss of centralization and decentralization are analyzed. The relationship between enterprise centralization and decentralization is discussed using a practical case. It is explained that either decentralization without limitation or over-centralization is acceptable. Basis and standards to deal with the relationship of centralization and decentralization are presented.
