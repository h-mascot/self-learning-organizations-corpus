# Analysis on Two Thoughts on the Central and Local Decentralization Before and After the 8th CPC National Congress

## Evidence span

> China is a big multi-ethnic developing country with imbalanced development among different regions.The central and local decentralization is not only an important theoretical issue but also a pressing practical issue.After the basic completion of socialist transformation,to change the highly centralized management system,the central government started to decentralize the power to the local governments.Two patterns of decentralization have been formed in practices,namely the gradual decentralization and radical decentralization,with different results for two patterns.The practices provide historical experiences and lessons for dealing with the central and local decentralization issues at present days.
