# Meta's Hyperagents & Self-Correcting Agents

## Verified source excerpt

> “_The feedback loop, not the function, is the unit of intelligence._”
> 
> 
> Norbert Wiener, [Cybernetics](https://direct.mit.edu/books/oa-monograph/4581/Cybernetics-or-Control-and-Communication-in-the) (1948)

One of the many [skills](https://jdsemrau.substack.com/p/the-absurd-effectiveness-of-skillsmd) I have implemented for “Sentinel” fka Superbill is a three-scenario intrinsic value model ([Investopedia](https://www.investopedia.com/articles/basics/12/intrinsic-value.asp)).

This is a very common workflow that aims to identify if the stock is valued correctly or not.

Partway through the analysis, the skill called the “_calculate\_irr_” tool to retrieve deterministically MOIC figures. In case you don’t know, Multiple on Invested Capital is a measure of how many times over an investor has returned their initial investment.

What then happened surprised me. And I am not ashamed to disclose that I did not expect that behaviour, even though I designed the agent.
