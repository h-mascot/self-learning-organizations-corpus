# Goal 1 — World's Largest Self-Learning Organizations Corpus

## North star
Build and verify the largest publicly discoverable GitHub corpus specifically devoted to self-learning, self-improving, AI-native, adaptive, and recursively improving organizations.

## Hard completion gates
- 100+ validated relevant YouTube videos with complete timestamped transcripts.
- Missing seed `I9c8STV7Hnw` ingested; unrelated `fVut0ceg2IY` quarantined and excluded from totals.
- Sources organized by platform: YouTube, arXiv, X, Reddit, Substack, blogs, podcasts, conferences, books, case studies.
- Every resource uses title-derived filename: `<date>--<normalized-title>--<publisher>--<stable-id>.md`.
- Existing ID-only filenames migrated with manifest and links updated.
- Pedro Franceschi/Brex and Ramp research lanes completed.
- Actual Perplexity and OpenAI/GPT deep-research artifacts preserved with citations, or exact verified credential/tool blocker documented after recovery attempts.
- 200 productive recursive research loops, each with evidence and ledger entry.
- Competitive GitHub corpus benchmark supports scoped largest claim; otherwise keep collecting until surpassed or produce exact hard blocker.
- Repository tooling validates provenance, metadata, relevance, dedupe, transcript completeness, rights status, naming and generated statistics.
- CI passes; changes committed, pushed, and verified on live GitHub.

## Operating doctrine
- Preserve raw evidence; derived intelligence is regenerable.
- Primary sources over hype. Marketing claims labeled.
- No material claim without source span.
- No source counted before topical/transcript validation.
- Copyright and platform rights recorded; do not imply ownership.
- Failures trigger diagnosis, fallback, checkpoint, and retry.
- Workers use isolated branches/worktrees and commit coherent batches.
- Manager merges, resolves conflicts, validates counts, pushes, and audits completion.

## Required research taxonomy
Organizational learning, learning organizations, continuous improvement, lean/kaizen, cybernetics, adaptive enterprises, AI-native companies, autonomous companies, company brains, organizational memory, agentic organizations, recursive improvement, continual-learning agents, automated experimentation, self-optimizing products, agent monitoring/repair, eval-driven development, data flywheels, decision intelligence, organizational digital twins.

## Worker contract
Each worker must keep `research/progress/<lane>.md`, commit changes, and return commit SHA plus concrete counts and validation output. No prose-only completion.
